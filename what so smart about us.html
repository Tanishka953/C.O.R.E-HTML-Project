About our AI Powered Security System

Our AI security systems are very advanced, safe and are shielded from all types of hacks! When you enter your home, our AI uses a fingerprint scanner to verify your fingerprint to verify if it is you or some other stranger trying to enter your house. You can also put the fingerprints of all your fingers and both hands! After this setting, you can use any finger to open your door with no problem! After our fingerprint scan, the AI analyzes your face, eyes and walking pattern. It has a motion sensor which gets activated as soon as you step inside your property. As soon as you step into your property, it starts to scan your face, eyes, and movement pattern. Each AI is fed with all the information about you(the owner of the house) before it is fully activated. We also have invisible motion sensors set up round your entire house which are controlled by the AI. Whenever someone is entering from the back, front, left right or from any direction, the motion sensors get activated and the AI is alerted about it. As soon as the AI detects a person trying to break in to your house, it activates face recognition which detects who is trying to enter your house. They alert you and if you approve the person from entering your home, no safety measures are activated. If they are not approved, then several security measures are switched on one by one.
